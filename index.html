The Company

Maintenance has arrived! is a Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning business utilizing training and tailored solutions to maintain a clean environment at your home or business.

The Cleaning Process

  • Introduction
  • Pile Preparation
  • Chemical Application
  • Hot Water Extraction
  • Clean and Finalise

Introduction:

We ask you to introduce us to yourself and your cleaning requirements. The better we can understand your specific needs, the better we can tailor a solution.

Pile Preparation (Optional):

If your carpet/Upholstery is medium to heavily soiled then we will vacuum the carpet as required. You can save money by making sure yours is already done.

Chemical Application:

Chemical spray specific to your requirements is applied on all carpet/upholstery surfaces. There is a range of products specifically selected to meet environmentally safe standards.

Hot water extraction:

Hot water is locally forced through the carpet/upholstery pile to the base of the fibres. The dirty water is subsequently sucked out by a high pressure vacuum. This task is performed by industry specific hardware that is designed and developed here in Australia.

Spot Treatment:

Stains requiring additional attention are spot treated with a carefully selected chemical. This is followed by additional hot water extraction.

Cleanup:

Any Furniture with timber feet are capped to avoid staining.
